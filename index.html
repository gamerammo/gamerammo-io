Gamerammo — The blockchain asset that gets it

What is Gamerammo?

Gamerammo is different that any other crypto asset because it provides a dedicated space and use case for gaming. It operates based on the premise that we are all in competition with each other—this is what makes us gamers. It's how we win in game and IRL.

Why should you 'get some'?

Gamerammo is completely dependent on its gamers. While other crypto assets are designed to impact the currency markets, that doesn't matter to us. Gamerammo sits within the gamerchain, a blockchain that is fueled entirely by gamers and built to last with Ethereum Classic.

Voice — Impact Content

With Voice, you'll be able to directly impact the content you see. You get ten per day to use and their burned the next. Choose what you produce and support wisely.

gamerammo — The Currency

Gamerammo is everything you want from a crypto asset but is tailor-made for gamers. Use it to support other gamers, increase your Voice, buy Armory items, or take it to market.

Float — Useable Exchange

Float is translateable into real world currency. Float represents the distribution of real world currency based on your input and impact while using the platform.

Gaming is a profession

We believe that gaming should be a sustainable and respected profession. Everything we do will aim to push that ideal forward on a platform built for this profession.

Ethereum Classic

We’re building our platform on the Ethereum Classic blockchain because gamers come first. On Etereum Classic, code is law. The same is true for the Gamerchain.

Blockchain Gamerchain
